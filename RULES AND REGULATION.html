        RULES AND REGULATION

Dear Delegate,
It is our esteemed honor to host you in this virtual edition of Sri Sri University International Model United Nations Conference, 2021 which will be held virtually under the title of Sri Sri University on 20th and 21st February 2021.
For mutual convenience of the delegate and the organizing committee, following are the codes of conduct and certain guidelines which have to be followed during the two day conference.

Registration and deadlines

1. The registrations are accepted on a first come, first serve basis. Once the capacity is reached, you will be waitlisted and moved to the next committee.
2. Allotments of countries and committees will be made on the basis of availability and the experience of delegates.
3. No registrations will be accepted after the deadline. Allowing registrations under special circumstances after the deadline will be decided by the secretariat and Delegate affairs team only.

Cancellation Policy

1. The registration fees once paid is non-refundable under any circumstances.
2. If the delegate misses any session, he/she will be responsible for it. No refund will be issued.
3. No refunds will be provided to delegates who are disqualified or to those who quit for any reason.

Code of conduct

1. Strict disciplinary actions will be taken against any misconduct or misbehavior during the sessions.
2. You certify that all the information submitted is authentic and any forgery or falsifications encountered by SSUIMUN will lead to immediate termination of your participation.
3. You certify that all the work submitted during the conference will be original. Plagiarism is inacceptable and any traces found by the Secretariat will lead to immediate disqualification without any refund.
4. Fellow delegates and Executive board members must be treated respectfully with courtesy.
5. SSUIMUN issues only one participation certificate per delegate. Delegates must download the E- certificates as soon as they receive it. SSUIMUN does not re-issue certificates.
