ESR Activities

We not only conducts conferences and seminars modeled as per the United Nations but also collaborates with different NGOs to fight against different social issues. At SSU International MUN we believe that just deliberation would not be enough so we go out in the actual world and take initiatives to make a difference in the world and in the lives of people one edition at a time.

Skill development Projects

Women form a major part of our society and empowering them is what we believe in. We have taken up rural training projects for women named “Pattachitra” in association with the Government of Odisha.

Children's Welfare Initiatives

We were elated to have partnered with Ms. Megha Bhatia - the founder of Our Voix. Along with working on the prevention of child sexual abuse, many workshops on Mind, Health, Time Management were also conducted under this initiative.

Women's health and safety Projects

SSUIMUN has indulged in “PAVITRA” workshops aimed at enhancing awareness about adolescent menstrual health and hygiene. Women self-defense workshops have also been conducted under the same Project.

Poverty and cleanliness Projects

Pop-Up housing for Humanity - An initiative by Mr. Sampath Reddy uses recyclable industrial materials for building modular portable housing.
We follow the mission “No Child Sleeps on the Floor.”
Numerous cleanliness drives were also conducted as we understand the importance of cleanliness and maintain it forever to continue a healthy and peaceful life.
