About SSUIMUN: - 

SSUIMUN is a simulation of United Nations hosted under the title of Sri Sri University. Ever since its foundation in 2017, we have carried the passion and potential to stand out in this world. SSUIMUN as a project aims at promoting the views and values of the United Nations. We strive to bring curious and creative minds from all over the world under one roof and to create world leaders for change and bring out the best.We not only conduct conferences and seminars modeled as per the United Nations but also collaborate with different NGOs to fight against different social issues.

We provide a platform which helps youth transcend their boundaries and allows them to see everything in a new light, which has made it easy for them to find the will power to go beyond the natural environment and become more. With this edition of our conference, we believe to create an impact in lives that will be remembered for a long time to come.
